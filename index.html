Radish Microgreens Benefits:

Rich in vitamins A, C, and K, these microgreens support liver detoxification, digestion, and offer a peppery flavor. They grow quickly (7-14 days) and are perfect for salads and sandwiches.

Sunflower Microgreens : Benefits

Packed with vitamins A, B, D, E, and proteins, they support heart health, skin, and immunity. They grow in 7-10 days and add a nutty flavor to salads and smoothies.

Pea Shoot Microgreens : Benefits

Full of vitamins A, C, and K, they support bone health and digestion. With a sweet, fresh taste, they grow in 7-14 days and are great in salads and sandwiches.

Basil Microgreens : Benefits

Contain antioxidants, vitamins A, C, and K, and have anti-inflammatory properties. They grow in 10-14 days and are ideal for Mediterranean dishes, soups, and pizzas.
